LOGISTICS CENTER
On the Airena, there are three giant towers: "Sentinel," "Watcher," and "Overseer." They are the core of the island's defense, the control center for androids, and the supply management hub. These towers are crucial during Airena's Oculus Principi transformation process. When the "Sentinel" was blown up by the Anonymous, the island's defense system became compromised. Yet, for some unknown reason, the Oculus Principi started the Airena Trials before being fully repaired.
"Awakener" means those who awaken before others. Within Oculus Principi, it refers to people who can sync with GenLink and acquire special powers.

After syncing, Awakeners are reborn with new abilities fueled by Source. However, GenSphere also acts as a second heart for them, so they constantly need more Sources to keep it going. As a result, Awakeners must rely on the Oculus Principi, participating in trials or missions to keep their Source levels up.
