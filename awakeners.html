Elite Pilot Profiles in Fate Trigger

Fate Trigger's Awakeners are the elite pilots who have mastered the art of mecha combat. Each Awakener brings unique abilities, specialized skills, and tactical advantages to the battlefield. Understanding their backgrounds, abilities, and optimal playstyles is crucial for success in competitive Fate Trigger matches.

As enigmatic as the night and as tricky as a puzzle. Huxley was born to be a spy, and anyone who tries to manipulate her will end up as the dumbest piece on the board.

Special Abilities

Realm of Mist
Awakeners who find themselves in the Realm of Mist will have their vision obscured, leaving Huxley with clear vision. The mist hides the steps of its shadowy mistress.
Mark of Rouge
Among the Orbis Cognitio's many gadgets, the rouge marker is Huxley's favorite. Combining versatility and ease of use, the rouge reveals Huxley's enemies and ensures that she is the picture of lethal beauty.
Coup de Grace
The Shadow's abilities as an interrogator are well-known and widely feared. When Huxley finishes off a downed enemy, their squadmates' locations are revealed to her.

Red Nightfall

Mindy's Gift

A new rising star from the ocean, a guide favored by the sea. Nase carries on her captain father's legacy, ready to bring the miracle of the Green Delphina miracles back to the world.

Special Abilities

Tide Whisper
Nase becomes one with the ocean, becoming Blessed for a duration. If Nase takes lethal damage while Blessed, she transforms into a body of water that can move freely and continuously recovers HP. While transformed, Nase cannot attack or use abilities, and reverts to her original form after a period of time.
Sharkie Bomb
Nase summons Sharkie, who moves forward and leaves a trail of water. When Sharkie encounters an enemy or reaches his max range, he jumps up and detonates, knocking up enemies struck.

The will to defend carries over from the virtual world into the reality in crisis. This time, Kira is not just a player but also an Awakener and a hero.

Special Abilities

Digital Fortress
Kira creates a holographic two-storey Fortress. The Fortress comes with four surrounding jump pads that lead to the top floor. Each wall of the Fortress can be destroyed separately. When Kira leaves the Fortress for a time, it collapses automatically.
Holo-Shield
Kira creates a holographic composite shield. The shield is made up of four parts, and each part can be destroyed separately. Allied fire can pass through the top shield. Up to four shields can exist at once.
Energy Matrix
When Kira is in her own Digital Fortress, she is protected by it. Digital Fortress continuously restores its creator's Source Energy and Armor.
