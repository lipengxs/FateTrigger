The World of Fate Trigger

The World of Fate Trigger

AWAKENER

"Awakener" means those who awaken before others. Within Oculus Principi, it refers to people who can sync with GenLink and acquire special powers.

After syncing, Awakeners are reborn with new abilities fueled by Source. However, GenSphere also acts as a second heart for them, so they constantly need more Sources to keep it going. As a result, Awakeners must rely on the Oculus Principi, participating in trials or missions to keep their Source levels up.

OCULUS PRINCIPI

Evolution is the key to unlocking the future, and it's the Oculus Principi, with its broader perspective, that carries this important mission.

Oculus Principi was an organization established by the first Awakener among humans. They're a secretive, massive organization, with their influence quietly affecting the entire world. They've inherited ancient human technology and have a deeper understanding of how to use Source, developing tech that's ahead of its time. They see Awakeners as the next step in human evolution. Everything they do, including Project Novita, is all about achieving that ultimate goal...

HELIX VITALIS

Ever since the sparks of civilization began to glow, humanity has set a relentless pace of exploring the unknowns. Bit by bit, they have uncovered the mysteries surrounding the ancient Aeternums scattered throughout the planet.

First among them was the Aeternum in Nordhelden, long before it received its present monikerâ€”Helix Vitalis. It was buried underground, exposing only the kaleidoscopic Helix Core that enraptured the imagination. People quickly built the Aeternum Research Complex around the Core, gathering the best scientific minds around the world to study it. But humanity was unaware that an unprecedented challenge was silently approaching...

PALE BLIGHT

On the crumbling, distorted land, gray-white waves sweep across, devouring everything.

To most people, the Pale Blight is just a disaster from the Transfigured Aeternum, but there's a hidden truth behind it. The Oculus Principi secretly activated the Aeternum in Vastas to collect enough Sources for Project Novita, but they didn't expect it to cause such a huge disaster. As soon as the Aeternum activated, the Helix Vitalis erupted from the ground, with its core rising and its roots spreading out, greedily absorbing everything around it. Anything it drains turns gray and decays, eventually becoming part of the Pale Blight. As long as the Helix Vitalis' core keeps drawing energy and converting it into Source, the Pale Blight will keep spreading, like human greed that never stops.

AIRENA

In the disaster's core, a forbidden zone unreachable by most, a trial of evolution is unfolding.

Unlike the land ravaged by the Pale Blight, the area around the Aeternum Research Complex has been lifted into the air by the ever-expanding Helix Vitalis, forming several Airenas that display all sorts of strange sights within the cycle of the Helix Vitalis. To uncover the secrets of the Helix Vitalis and enhance Awakeners' GenSync level, the Oculus Principi took control of the Airena and regularly sent Awakeners there for combat trials. This is where the journey of Awakeners begins.

The Battlefields of Fate Trigger

The Fate Trigger universe spans across multiple star systems, each hosting unique battlefields that reflect the diverse environments and conflicts within the game world. These locations are strategically important in the ongoing struggle for dominance.

Battlefield Mastery in Fate Trigger

Each battlefield in Fate Trigger represents a piece of this larger universe, with its own history, strategic importance, and connection to the ongoing narrative. Understanding not just the tactical layouts but also the world context of each location will enhance your mastery of Fate Trigger combat. From urban environments to alien landscapes, mastering these maps is crucial for success in competitive mecha combat.

Primary Battlefield

Helix Arena

The iconic central battlefield of Fate Trigger, Helix Arena represents one of the most strategically important locations in the Core Systems. This massive orbital facility serves as a neutral ground where factions conduct formalized combat trials. The unique spiral design reflects the architectural philosophy of the early space colonizers, combining form and function in a structure that has witnessed countless decisive battles in Fate Trigger history.

Key Features

  • Multi-level spiral architecture
  • Central control point
  • Elevated sniper positions
  • Multiple flanking routes
  • Dynamic cover systems

Strategic Tips

Control the central high ground to dominate Helix Arena. Use the spiral design to your advantage by maintaining elevation and controlling sight lines. The multiple levels allow for creative flanking maneuvers in Fate Trigger combat.

Urban Combat

Neo-Tokyo District

Neo-Tokyo District stands as a testament to humanity's resilience and adaptability. Once a thriving metropolitan center, this city became a battleground when Corporate Alliance forces attempted to seize control of its advanced manufacturing facilities. The dense urban environment now hosts some of the most intense close-quarters combat in Fate Trigger, with every building and alleyway telling a story of the conflict that continues to shape this world.

Key Features

  • Dense urban environment
  • Destructible buildings
  • Underground tunnels
  • Rooftop combat areas
  • Multiple spawn points

Strategic Tips

Utilize the verticality of buildings for tactical advantages. The underground tunnels provide excellent flanking routes, while rooftops offer superior positioning for ranged combat in Fate Trigger.

Industrial Zone

Foundry Complex

The Foundry Complex represents the industrial heart of the Fate Trigger universe, where millions of tons of raw materials are processed daily to fuel the ongoing war effort. Control of this facility determines which faction can produce Strikers and weaponry at scale. The hazardous environments reflect both the dangerous nature of heavy industry and the deliberate sabotage attempts by competing factions seeking to disrupt enemy supply chains.

Key Features

  • Industrial machinery
  • Hazardous zones
  • Conveyor belt systems
  • High-temperature areas
  • Mechanical obstacles

Strategic Tips

Learn to use industrial machinery as cover and leverage environmental hazards against opponents. The conveyor systems can be used for rapid repositioning during Fate Trigger battles.

Alien Environment

Xenon Prime

Xenon Prime represents humanity's expansion into truly alien territories. This mysterious world, discovered during the Frontier Expansion period, challenges everything pilots know about combat in Fate Trigger. The low-gravity zones and exotic alien crystal formations suggest advanced technology from a previous civilization. Factions fight not just for territory here, but for the technological secrets buried within this world, making Xenon Prime one of the most strategically valuable locations in the Fate Trigger universe.

Key Features

  • Low-gravity zones
  • Alien crystal formations
  • Exotic energy fields
  • Floating platforms
  • Unique physics

Strategic Tips

Adapt your combat style to the low-gravity environment. Use floating platforms for aerial combat and leverage alien crystal formations for cover. The unique physics create new tactical opportunities in Fate Trigger.

The Universe of Fate Trigger

Culture & Society

In the Fate Trigger universe, society has adapted to a reality where mecha combat determines political outcomes. Pilots are revered as heroes and strategic assets, while corporations and governments compete to recruit the best talent. The fusion of military tradition and technological innovation has created a unique culture where honor, skill, and tactical brilliance are highly valued across all factions.

Technology & Advancements

The Fate Trigger world features technology beyond current imagination. Strikers represent the pinnacle of mecha engineering, combining powerful weapons systems, advanced AI assistance, and energy shields. Research stations continuously develop new technologies, and breakthroughs can shift the balance of power dramatically. The constant innovation drives the competitive nature of Fate Trigger battles.

Conflict History

The current state of conflict in Fate Trigger stems from decades of territorial disputes, resource competition, and ideological differences. What began as exploration and colonization efforts evolved into a complex web of alliances and rivalries. Major historical events, including the Great Schism and the Resource Wars, continue to influence faction relationships and battlefield strategies today.

Future Evolution

As Fate Trigger continues to evolve, new worlds are discovered, new technologies emerge, and new conflicts arise. The universe is designed to expand, with future updates planned to introduce additional battlefields, factions, and story elements. Players' actions in Fate Trigger battles contribute to the ongoing narrative, making every match part of a larger story.

Fate Trigger World Timeline

2095-2120

The Great Expansion

Humanity begins colonizing nearby star systems, establishing the foundation for the Fate Trigger universe. Early space settlements and resource extraction operations create the first interplanetary conflicts.

2125-2140

Mecha Technology Revolution

The first Striker prototypes are developed, revolutionizing warfare and exploration. These early mecha units become the backbone of military operations across the Fate Trigger universe.

2145-2150

The Great Schism

Major factions split from the United Terran Federation, leading to the establishment of the Corporate Alliance, Free Colonies, and Order of Technologists. This event shapes the political landscape of Fate Trigger.

2152-2156

The Resource Wars

Intense conflicts erupt over control of newly discovered resource-rich worlds. These wars establish the strategic importance of various locations featured in Fate Trigger battles.

2157 (Present)

The Current Era

The present-day setting of Fate Trigger, where organized combat trials and strategic battles determine faction dominance. Players participate in this ongoing conflict, shaping the future of the Fate Trigger universe.
