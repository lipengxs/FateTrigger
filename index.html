About Fate Trigger

Fate Trigger is a groundbreaking mecha battle game that combines strategic thinking with fast-paced action in an immersive multiplayer environment. Set in the year 2157, humanity has colonized multiple star systems and developed advanced mecha technology for both exploration and warfare. The fate of entire civilizations now rests in the hands of skilled pilots who can master these incredible machines known as "Strikers".

Story & Background

In Fate Trigger, players take control of powerful robotic combat units and engage in epic battles across diverse battlefields. The game features a rich narrative where different factions compete for control of valuable resources and strategic territories. Each battle in Fate Trigger carries weight, as victories can shift the balance of power in this futuristic universe.

Release Information

Fate Trigger was officially released in 2024 and is now available on PC (Steam), PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S platforms. The game supports cross-platform play, allowing players to battle against opponents regardless of their chosen platform. Regular updates and new content ensure that the Fate Trigger experience continues to evolve.

Supported Languages

Fate Trigger supports multiple languages including English, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Korean, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, and Russian, ensuring players worldwide can enjoy the full experience with proper localization. The comprehensive language support in Fate Trigger makes it accessible to a global audience, fostering an international community of mecha combat enthusiasts.

Progressive Customization

The progression system in Fate Trigger rewards both skill and dedication.

Frequently Asked Questions About Fate Trigger

What is Fate Trigger and when was it released?

How does the mecha customization system work in Fate Trigger?
