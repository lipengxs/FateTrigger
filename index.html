About Fate Trigger

Fate Trigger is a groundbreaking mecha battle game that combines strategic thinking with fast-paced action in an immersive multiplayer environment. The fate of entire civilizations now rests in the hands of skilled pilots who can master these incredible machines known as "Strikers".
