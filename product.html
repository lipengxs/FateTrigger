Product Information

Everything you need to know about playing Dune: Awakening

System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 or AMD Ryzen 5 2600X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB or AMD Radeon RX 580 8GB
  • Storage: 100 GB available space
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection

Recommended Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10/11 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-10700K or AMD Ryzen 7 5800X
  • Memory: 32 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3070 or AMD Radeon RX 6800
  • Storage: 100 GB SSD
  • Network: High-speed Internet connection

About the Publisher

Funcom is a leading developer and publisher of online games for PC and consoles. Founded in 1993, the company has developed and published numerous acclaimed titles, including 'Conan Exiles', 'Age of Conan', 'The Secret World', and many more.

With over 25 years of experience in the gaming industry, Funcom continues to innovate and create engaging experiences for players worldwide.
